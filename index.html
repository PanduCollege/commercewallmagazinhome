Commerce Department
Wall Magazine

The Wall Magazine of Commerce Department is inaugurated by respected Vice-Principal Dr. Chandana Sinha Roy, in presence faculties and students of the department. The theme of the Wall Magazine was "Tourism of Assam", where major tourist spots of Assam, are presented through it. In the inauguration ceremony, respected Vice-Principal madam has addressed to all after inauguration and explain how this type of works helps the students in participative learning and also encouraged the students and faculty to continue sach type of activities in upcoming time regularly. She also appreciated the students for such a beautiful presentation of the Wall Magazine.
